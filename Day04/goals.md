# Day 05 — Goals

1. Understand Namespaces as isolation boundaries — what they isolate and what they do not
2. Create Namespaces for multiple simulated teams and apply ResourceQuotas to each
3. Observe ResourceQuota enforcement — what happens when a team exceeds their quota
4. Understand LimitRange — how it sets default resource requests and limits per container
5. Understand RBAC — Roles, ClusterRoles, RoleBindings, ClusterRoleBindings
6. Create a Role that grants read-only access to Pods in one namespace
7. Create a ServiceAccount and bind a Role to it
8. Verify RBAC enforcement — confirm restricted access works as configured
9. Understand the difference between Role and ClusterRole scope
