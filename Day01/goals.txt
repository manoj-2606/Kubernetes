Day 01 Goals — Kubernetes Foundations

1. Understand the Kubernetes architecture: control plane components and worker node components
2. Understand the reconciliation loop (desired state vs actual state)
3. Install kubectl and minikube on Windows (PowerShell)
4. Bootstrap a single-node local cluster with minikube
5. Understand the difference between imperative and declarative approaches
6. Create a Namespace, deploy a Pod imperatively, then replace it with a declarative Deployment
7. Observe rollout behavior and understand why Deployments exist over raw Pods
