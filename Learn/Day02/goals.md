# Day 02 — Goals

1. Understand why Pod IPs are unreliable and why Services exist
2. Understand Labels and Selectors — how Services find Pods
3. Deploy a ClusterIP Service and verify internal Pod-to-Pod routing
4. Deploy a NodePort Service and access the application from outside the cluster
5. Understand what happens to traffic routing when a Pod is deleted and replaced
6. Understand the Endpoints object — what it is and how it stays updated
7. Observe kube-proxy behavior through Service routing in practice
